CrazyApe.exe
-------------------------------------------------------------
The aim of the game is to move the monkey one after the 
other, collect all 3 bananas and bring them to the house,
with the trees being an obstacle.
The monkey has 5 lives.
By collecting hearts you can get back your lost lives.
When touching a scorpion or a brown coconut
1 life is deducted, a red coconut 3 lives
and when touching the tiger the monkey dies
and the game starts from the beginning, by pressing the
Play-Button.
